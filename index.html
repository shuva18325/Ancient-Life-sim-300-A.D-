Guard: your stance is your guard zone. Match the foe's stance to block their blow; mismatch and you eat steel. Watch their crest tilt — high crest = high attack.
Crowd: aggression, combos and clean blocks please the mob. Running and turtling bores them. The crowd meter decides mercy when a fighter falls — and how much Glory & Popularity you earn.
Stamina: every swing, hop and thrust costs wind. Rest on the ground to recover.
